Our Firm

About Fuller Architecture

Fuller Architecture was established in 1992 by John M. Fuller, AIA. The firm is located on the beautiful Court Square in downtown Mocksville, North Carolina, in the historic Southern Bank building.

The mission of Fuller Architecture will always be to provide our clients with quality design and service — just ask our clients! From the beginning, our firm has focused on a specialty in the design of religious facilities, having now designed over 50 church projects. Other developed specialties include financial, educational, civic, commercial and planning projects.

  • Historic restoration & sensitive additions
  • New construction & adaptive reuse
  • Master planning & town visioning
  • Personal attention on every project
